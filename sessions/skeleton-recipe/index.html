Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 100g fresh pasta (Preferably Spaghetti)
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g parmasean cheese
  • 100g Garlic Roule Cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • White Pepper
  • Two slices of brunswick ham

Method

  1. Heat a pan of water, add a glug of olive oil and add a good pinch of salt.
  2. once boiled add the pasta, stiring gently every 5 minutes to make sure it doesn't stick to the pan.
  3. Once the pasta is cooking grate the parmasean cheese and slice the ham into strips.
  4. Fresh pasta pasta should take about 5-8 minutes. Once aldente drain the pasta and return to the pan.
  5. Crack the two eggs onto the pasta and stir, letting the egg cook from the residual heat of the pasta.
  6. Add the Parmasean and Ham and crumble the roule in and mix well. you can put a very gentle heat under the pan to ensure the cheese is mixed into the sauce and the egg is cooked if you like.
  7. Serve with a generous sprinkle of black and white pepper.
  8. Enjoy!
